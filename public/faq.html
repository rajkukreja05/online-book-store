Shipping, payments, returns, and your account

Add books to cart, go to checkout, enter delivery details, then pay. You can track your orders from My Orders.
For this demo store, prepaid checkout is enabled. In a real deployment, COD can be added as an extra payment method.
Most metros: 2–4 days. Other locations: 4–7 days. See Shipping Info.
Easy 30-day returns for damaged or incorrect items. See Returns for steps.
Tap the heart on any book card to save it. Your wishlist is stored in your browser and available on the Wishlist page.
Share your invite link. Friends get a discount and you earn wallet credit after their first paid order (see the referral section on Home).
