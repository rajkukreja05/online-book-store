Returns

Simple steps to return or replace an item

Eligibility

Returns are accepted within 30 days for damaged items, incorrect items, or missing pages/printing defects.

How to request a return

  1. Open My Orders and locate your order.
  2. Contact support with your order id and issue.
  3. We arrange pickup (where available) or share the return address.

Refunds

Once your return is approved, refunds are processed back to the original payment method or wallet (as per checkout rules).
